FAQ

Questions before printing?

These answers help visitors understand the process before they request a quote.

What is the maximum print height?

The calculator is limited to 4 meters in height. The width is open, so long horizontal walls can still be estimated.

Can I upload my own image?

Yes. The demo allows visitors to upload an image and preview it on the wall as if the machine is printing it.

Can the background be removed?

The demo includes automatic light-background removal for uploaded images. For real production, artwork can be prepared professionally before printing.

What surfaces are best?

Smooth painted interior walls and prepared panels usually give the cleanest result. Rough surfaces may need testing or preparation.
